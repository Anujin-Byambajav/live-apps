Here is an Javascript application that I created using ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript

Population Density Map of Mongolia in the year of 1990-2013

This application shows the population density of Mongolia in the year of 1990 to 2013. When the application starts, it shows the total 2013 population density of Mongolia rendered in DotDensityRenderer. Each dot represents 100 people and it is distributed randomly across the areas or in this case, the soums. Soums are the second level administrative division after aimags(provinces). It is similar to a county in the United States. There are 331 soums in Mongolia. On the right side, the chart displays the total population for each year in Mongolia. I queried the total population of soums in the layer using the statistic query by defining the statisticType property. The chart also allows the user to see the male, the female or both populations distinctively.
